## Soft notes and future polish backlog

Not failures; flagging for a polish backlog.

1. **Keyboard-centric copy on touch viewports.** On a 390px touch device the first-visit hint reads "Arrow keys to walk and turn / Enter to view a painting / Esc to step back" and the focus prompt reads "Press Enter to view" (`home-390.png`, `mobile-tap-focused-390.png`); the story page back link shows an "Esc" badge (`mobile-story-390.png`). None of these inputs exist on a phone. Suggest viewport- or pointer-conditional copy ("Tap a painting to view", "Use the buttons to walk").
2. **Hint overlay collides with the touch cluster and never auto-dismisses on touch.** The hint card overlaps the Walk forward/back buttons visually (`home-390.png`, `mobile-hint-overlap-390.png`). Buttons still win hit-testing (verified by elementFromPoint and a real CDP touch), so it is cosmetic, but the hint stayed visible after button presses and canvas taps; only the close button or a keypress dismisses it. Suggest dismissing on first touch-control press and moving the card above the cluster.
3. **Tap-to-focus ignores the proximity rule.** A tap focused and opened a painting roughly 20 units away (tap at spawn reached the Ramayana wall painting). Desktop focus requires distance < 3.0. Probably intentional raycast behavior, but it means phone users never walk; worth a product decision.
4. **Carousel dot lags the keypress.** After the first ArrowRight, the active dot updated only after the smooth scroll finished (sampled active=0 with scrollLeft=100 at +800ms under software rendering). Minor; consider updating the dot from the keypress, not the scroll position.
5. **Em dash in the fallback copy.** "The collection is still open — browse every story in the flat gallery instead" (`nowebgl-1280.png`). Repo convention is no em dashes.
6. **Tradition colors and night lighting hold up** everywhere checked: amber ROME label, emerald RAMAYANA, blue Mahabharata chips on cards, gold NEW tags and readable placard on Wall 4 (`face-roman-1280.png`, `face-curator-1280.png`, `gallery-390.png`). Typography hierarchy clear; no near-invisible text found. Hover/active states present on the new touch buttons (`active:` classes) and the fallback gallery button.

## Overall verdict
