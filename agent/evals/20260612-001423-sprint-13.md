## Sprint goal judgment

The deliverable is what was asked for: invisible, honest, ghost-proof plumbing. Ghost-proofing is proven by interaction in all four modes (clean bye 630 ms, no-bye timeout 6.4 s, reload storm never overcounting, idle heartbeats holding 13 s). The HUD contract never lied at any sampled moment: `data-peers` matched live tabs, peer poses matched the source page's real HUD to 0.000 u, and `data-net` refused to claim `webrtc` with nothing connected. Solo behavior is byte-identical to sprint 12 in both degrade and opt-out modes, and presence costs zero network and zero pre-load JS where unwanted. The attribute stream (≈300 ms updates, maxStep 0.66 u under a 2-10 fps renderer) is dense and stable enough for sprint 14 to hang interpolated bodies on without protocol rework. Goal met, not letter-gamed.

## Soft notes (for sprints 14-16)

1. **`?net=` does not survive the story round trip.** Escape from a story lands on `/` with all query params stripped: a `?net=local` page silently switches to the production default transport (PROBE_URL_AFTER: `http://localhost:3000/`, `data-net="webrtc"` → degrade), so the two local pages never re-find each other and the returning page emits the default path's WS-failure noise. No sprint-13 AC covers this (rejoin is explicitly sprint 15 scope), but sprint 15's drafted "round-trip rejoin" AC will fail in local eval mode as-is. Fix direction: persist the chosen transport across the round trip (as cam mode already is) or carry the param through the Escape return.
2. **Same root cause:** after A's story trip, B showed `peers="0"` permanently (P7_B_AFTER_A_RETURNS) — despawn was clean, but there is no rejoin because A came back on a different transport. Expect sprint 15 to exercise this exact path.
3. **Visitor labels increment per session, not per human:** after A's reload storm, B labeled A's new session `Visitor 3`. Fine for the contract (AC1 only pins first join = `Visitor 2`), but in sprint 14 a rejoining human will visibly change number/color mid-visit. Worth a taste call in sprint 16.
4. **Observed pose-update cadence ≈300 ms under SwiftShader** (median MutationObserver gap 298 ms, medians 308-317 ms polled). Smooth on real hardware, but sprint 14's "no step > 0.5 u at 100 ms sampling" draft AC should stay displacement-anchored or it will flake in this sandbox.
5. **Default relay taper observed:** 5 distinct Nostr relays, 4 retry rounds, 20 failure logs ending 23.8 s — comfortably inside the amended bounds (<50, ≤45 s); the backoff behaves.
