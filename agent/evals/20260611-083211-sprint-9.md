## Soft notes (no gate impact; feed into Sprint 10 taste audit)

1. **"Esc" keyboard badge shows on touch devices.** The story page "Back to the museum" link renders an `Esc` key chip at 390 on a coarse pointer (shots/s9-mobile-prompt-390.png, shots/s9-carousel-swipe-390.png). AC1 scoped only the hint overlay and focus prompt, so this passes, but a keyboard hint on a phone is incongruous. Suggest hiding the chip under the same coarse-pointer condition used for the hint/prompt copy.
2. **Raw markdown emphasis in story body.** "The Armor Karna Gave Away" renders literal asterisks: "*kavacha*", "*kundala*" (shots/s9-carousel-swipe-390.png). Emphasis is not being parsed to `<em>`. Content-pipeline defect worth a taste-audit finding.
3. **Em dashes in story body copy.** E.g. "its widest stretch — from Scotland to Mesopotamia" (Marcus Aurelius story). AC4 scoped overlay/fallback text only, so no gate, but the repo voice rule says no em dashes; the generation skill may want a lint.
4. **Default 1280 spawn composition feels sparse.** From spawn the far wall's paintings read small with large empty floor/wall area mid-frame (shots/s9-home-default-1280.png). Candidate taste-audit item: tighter default spawn distance or larger painting scale.
5. Touch cluster, hint toast, and focus prompt stack cleanly at 390 with comfortable gaps; hint text legible over the bright room. Hover/focus states on dots present (accent border on active). No issues.
